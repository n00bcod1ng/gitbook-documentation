# Ticket #1 – Cannot Log In After Password Expiry

**Reported by:** Sarah Chen – Finance **Priority:** High **Category:** Account & Access **Status:** Closed

**Issue:** User was locked out of her workstation after her password expired. She attempted to reset it but received a complexity requirements error. Access was urgent due to month-end Finance reporting.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (12).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Troubleshooting & Resolution:** The account was located in Active Directory Users and Computers. The account was not locked — the password had simply expired. An admin password reset was performed with "User must change password at next logon" enabled. The temporary password was communicated via phone. User confirmed successful login within 15 minutes.
